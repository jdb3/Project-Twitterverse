Background

Twitter, one of the world's largest social media services, has now become a platform for politicians, organizations, and companies to give updates to their followers. Users representing a company or a political party use Twitter to state views on current news, push their political campaigns and even confirm official policy decisions. Top People have used the site to broadcast their thoughts to millions of followers including President Donald Trump, Tesla founder Elon Musk and Current UK Prime Minister Boris Johnson.

Hypothesis

When influental people tweet, the market moves. The President, CEOs, maybe even celebrities have enough influence that when they send out a tweet, there is a response in either the market as a whole or perhaps within their respective company's stock prices.

Goal

We will analyze past tweets to train a machine learning model to correlate past tweets with market movement, then use that model to predict the effect of future tweets on market response. Content, sentiment, followers and retweets can all be investigated as potential features that affect the response
